## Pushing My Git Repositories to SourceTube

[omg.lol](https://home.omg.lol/) [launched SourceTube](https://omglol.news/2025/11/20/say-hello-to-sourcetube-the-omg-lol-community-code-forge), a [Forgejo](https://forgejo.org/) instance for the omg.lol community.

I registered an account on SourceTube as soon as it launched, though I only started to push the Git repositories of my personal projects, including this website, to SourceTube. I love omg.lol, its community, and I have a lifetime membership, so I would like to make good use of omg.lol's services as much as possible.

Not to mention, while I am already hosting [my own instance of Gitea](https://git.helenchong.dev/helenchong), of which Forgejo is a fork of, it is a good idea to have multiple copies of your Git repositories just in case you lost one of the copies. Fortunately, you can configure your Git repositories to to multiple remote repositories at the same time.

You can check out [my SourceTube profile](https://source.tube/helenchong).
